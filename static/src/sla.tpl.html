Service-Level Agreement

Interested in commercial reuse of data from oaDOI? Check out our Service-Level Agreement (SLA). Here are the benefits:

  • You can download our whole database as a single file, and use it for commercial purposes.
  • You can get weekly updates on what changed in the oaDOI database. So, if you start with the download file, then subscribe to the weekly updates, you'll be able to maintain an exact copy of our database, on your own local servers.
  • You'll get enterprise-level service and support established and guaranteed in writing.
  • You help support oaDOI and keep it sustainable over the long term.

Of course, if you don't need these features, you can always just use the API; it's free to everyone for any use, and always will be.

Our pricing on the SLA is flexible, depending on your revenue and use-case. So if you're interested, please contact us; we'd love to hear from you!
